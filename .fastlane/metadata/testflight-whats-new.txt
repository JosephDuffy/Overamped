Overamped will automatically redirect AMP pages in Google search results and links opened from other apps.

New in beta 2:

- Enable allowing AMP for specific websites
- Support overriding AMP links for news articles
- Better detection of original non-AMP URL
- Loading more search results will redetect AMP links

Upcoming features:

- Support for Bing
- A revised icon
- A form to submit issues from within the extension
